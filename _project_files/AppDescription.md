# Glaze

**Tagline:** *The polished layer between your thoughts and the machine.*

## 1. Overview
**Glaze** is a premium, browser-based prompt management system designed for power users of AI agents. It recognizes that high-performance prompting often requires a bridge between languages—allowing users to store, refine, and organize their instructions in both German and English simultaneously. 

Built with a stunning **dark glassmorphism** interface, Glaze provides a distraction-free, aesthetically pleasing environment to build a permanent library of high-value AI templates. It serves as a central "base of operations" for your most effective prompt structures, ensuring they are always accessible and ready to use.

---

## 2. Core Features

### 📋 Template Management
* **Full CRUD Operations:** Create, Read, Update, and Delete prompt templates with ease.
* **Template Naming:** Assign unique names to every template (e.g., "Python Code Auditor" or "Blog Post Hook") for instant identification.

### 🌐 Bilingual Workspace
* **Side-by-Side Editor:** Dedicated text areas for German (DE) and English (EN) versions of a prompt.
* **Translation Bridge:** Integrated UI buttons for `DE -> EN` and `EN -> DE` translations (Visualized for future implementation).
* **Quick-Copy Icons:** Minimalist "one-click" buttons to copy content from either the DE or EN pane directly to your clipboard.

### 🏷️ Organization & Discovery
* **Metadata Tagging:** Add hashtag-style metadata (e.g., `#Coding`, `#Writing`, `#Logic`) to categorize templates.
* **Search & Filter:** A dedicated sidebar with a search bar to filter your library by keyword or tag.

---

## 3. Visual Identity (Glassmorphism)
Glaze utilizes a high-end "Vitreous" design language to minimize cognitive load and maximize focus:

* **Surfaces:** Semi-transparent "glass" panels with `backdrop-filter: blur(15px)` and thin 1px semi-transparent borders.
* **Background:** Deep midnight or charcoal base with subtle, flowing gradients.
* **Typography:** Clean, high-legibility sans-serif fonts (Inter or SF Pro).
* **Interactions:** Subtle neon-tinted glow effects for active text fields and successful copy actions.
* **Status States:** Distinct visual treatment for active vs. disabled (coming soon) features.

---

## 4. User Motivation
Modern AI workflows often involve thinking in one's native language (German) while leveraging the superior performance of certain models in English. Glaze eliminates the friction of switching tabs and losing track of prompt versions, providing a professional-grade repository for the daily use of AI agents.
